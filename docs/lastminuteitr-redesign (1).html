Questions

Straight answers before you start

Do you file my return for me?

No. We reconcile your documents, compare regimes, and generate a copy-ready guide. You submit and e-verify the return yourself, directly on incometax.gov.in.

Is this safe and lawful?

Yes. We only suggest deductions and regime choices permitted under the Income Tax Act, and we're DPDP compliant. We never auto-submit on your behalf.

What documents do I need?

Start with Form 16 and your AIS. If you have capital gains, you can also import statements from Groww or MFCentral for a fuller picture.

Old regime or new — which is better?

It depends on your deductions and income mix — there's no universal answer. Our comparator runs both on your actual numbers so you can decide with evidence, not a guess.

What do I pay for?

You pay to unlock the mismatch engine, regime comparison, and your personalised portal-filing guide. The actual filing and e-verification on incometax.gov.in is free, as it should be.
