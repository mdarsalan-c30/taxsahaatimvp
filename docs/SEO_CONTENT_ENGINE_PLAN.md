# SEO Content Engine Plan

## Goal
Drive organic traffic by answering common tax questions in simple English. 

## Strategy
- **Tone:** Beginner-friendly, Trustworthy, Simple English. No fake claims.
- **Core Keyword:** "Last minute ITR filing", "Save tax legally", "ITR AI assistant".
- **Blog Engine:** Programmatic SEO pages generated from a database of common tax questions.

## First 30 Priority Blogs (Examples)
1. How to file ITR at the last minute?
2. What happens if I miss the July 31 ITR deadline?
3. Old vs New Tax Regime: Which is better for a 10 Lakh Salary?
4. How to claim 80C deductions without proofs? (Answer: You can't, proofs are required for audit).
5. What is the standard deduction in the New Tax Regime?

## FAQ Schema & Glossary
Every blog post will include Google FAQ Schema and link to a central Tax Glossary (e.g., explaining terms like "Assessment Year", "Capital Gains", "Section 80D").
