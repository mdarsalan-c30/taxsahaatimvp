# Pricing Strategy

## B2C Pricing Plans

### 1. Normal (AI Filing Assistance)
- **Original Price:** ₹899
- **Launch Price:** ₹359
- **Positioning:** "For simple salaried users who want AI-guided filing checks."
- **Features:** Standard engine computation, pre-filled form 16, basic mismatch check.

### 2. Advance (Pro AI CA Assistance)
- **Original Price:** ₹1,299
- **Launch Price:** ₹499
- **Positioning:** "For users who want deeper AI checks, mismatch review, capital gains alerts, and priority companion guidance."
- **Features:** Everything in Normal + Active AI Companion queries, detailed mismatch resolution, priority support.

*Note: We avoided pricing both at ₹359 to prevent confusion. ₹499 for Pro establishes premium value.*

## B2B Bulk Plans (For CAs and HRs)

### Plan A: 20 Filings
- **Original:** ₹7,180
- **Launch:** ₹5,000

### Plan B: 40 Filings
- **Original:** ₹14,360
- **Launch:** ₹9,000

### Plan C: 100 Filings
- **Original:** ₹35,900
- **Launch:** ₹15,999

## Implementation
- **Credits System:** B2B users buy a plan and get "Credits" in their wallet. Each filing deducts 1 credit.
- **Payment Gateway:** Razorpay (to be integrated in 2-3 days).
